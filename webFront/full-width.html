For those unfamiliar with The Hangover series, it basically chronicles the story of three friends who throw the notoriously iconic ‘Vegas Bachelor Party’ for their friend. Due to a mixup with drugs, they end up partying the night away but waking up with no recollection of what happened that night. The big caveat to the plot is that the groom is missing, and the rest of the bachelor party spends the next day re-piecing what transpired that night. The movies thus far in the series have been somewhat entertaining and at times a bit overboard (I don’t suggest bringing kids to watch the movie) but for the most part they seem to celebrate the most recent generation’s affinity for binge drinking and regret.

The Hangover 3 will bring back the same cast for one last party in Las Vegas (the previous movie took place in Thailand) and will also include some new faces, such as John Goodman. Considering how much trouble the group got into last time they were in Vegas, the end to the franchise seems to promise the same drunken punch as the first two.
